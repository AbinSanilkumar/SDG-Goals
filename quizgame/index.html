Instructions

Welcome to the SDG Trivia game! Here are the rules:

  • Select the number of questions and the time limit for each question.
  • Choose between Singleplayer and Multiplayer modes.
  • Answer the questions related to Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
  • In Singleplayer mode, you will answer the questions one by one.
  • In Multiplayer mode:
    • Player 1 chooses their option using the keys 1, 2, 3, and 4.
    • Player 2 chooses their option using the keys 5, 6, 7, and 8.
  • Good luck and have fun!
