GOAL 16

PEACE, JUSTICE AND STRONG INSTITUTIONS

AIM

This goal promotes peaceful and inclusive societies, ensuring access to justice for all individuals. It emphasizes the importance of building effective, accountable institutions and reducing violence and corruption. The goal encourages participation in decision-making and the promotion of human rights.

TARGETS
  • 16.1: Reduce Violence Everywhere
  • 16.2: Protect Children From Abuse, Exploitation, Trafficking And Violence
  • 16.3: Promote The Rule Of Law And Ensure Equal Access To Justice
  • 16.4: Combat Organized Crime And Illicit Financial And Arms Flows
  • 16.5: Substantially Reduce Corruption And Bribery
  • 16.6: Develop Effective, Accountable And Transparent Institutions
  • 16.7: Ensure Responsive, Inclusive And Representative Decision-making
  • 16.8: Strengthen The Participation In Global Governance
  • 16.9: Provide Universal Legal Identity

CHALLENGES
  • Conflict
  • Corruption
  • Human Rights Violations

SOLUTIONS
  • Strengthen governance and rule of law: Promote transparency, accountability, and anti-corruption measures.
  • Support conflict resolution and peacebuilding: Engage communities in dialogue and reconciliation processes.
