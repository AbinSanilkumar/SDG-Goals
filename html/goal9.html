GOAL 9

INDUSTRY, INNOVATION AND INFRASTRUCTURE

AIM

This goal promotes sustainable industrialization and fosters innovation. It emphasizes the need for resilient infrastructure to support economic development, particularly in developing countries. The goal encourages investment in research and development, technological innovation, and sustainable practices in industries.

       

TARGETS
  • 9.1 : Develop Sustainable, Resilient And Inclusive Infrastructures
  • 9.2 : Promote Inclusive And Sustainable Industrialization
  • 9.3 : Increase Access To Financial Services And Markets
  • 9.4 : Upgrade All Industries And Infrastructures For Sustainability
  • 9.5 : Enhance Research And Upgrade Industrial Technologies
  • 9.6 : Facilitate Sustainable Infrastructure Development For Developing Countries
  • 9.7 : Support Domestic Technology Development And Industrial Diversification
  • 9.8 : Universal Access To Information And Communications Technology

CHALLENGES
  • Lack of Investment
  • Climate Change
  • Inequality

SOLUTIONS
  • Invest in resilient infrastructure: Prioritize sustainable transport and communication networks.
  • Encourage research and development: Foster public-private partnerships for innovation.

Students as Changemakers: Ignite Your Vision to Revolutionize Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure
  • Participate in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) programs.
  • Promote sustainability and green technology projects.
  • Organize innovation fairs or hackathons at school.
  • Advocate for modern and tech-enhanced learning environments.
  • Engage in public forums on urban planning and infrastructure.
  • Support entrepreneurship and startup initiatives.
