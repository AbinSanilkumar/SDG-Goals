GOAL 13

CLIMATE ACTION

AIM

Climate Action calls for urgent measures to combat climate change and its impacts. It emphasizes the need for resilience and adaptation strategies and promotes the integration of climate change measures into national policies. The goal highlights the importance of reducing greenhouse gas emissions and supporting climate finance.

TARGETS
  • 13.1: Strengthen Resilience and Adaptive Capacity to Climate Related Disasters.
  • 13.2: Integrate Climate Change Measures Into Policies and Planning.
  • 13.3: Build Knowledge and Capacity to Meet Climate Change.
  • 13.4: Implement the Un Framework Convention on Climate Change.
  • 13.5: Promote Mechanisms to Raise Capacity for Planning and Management.

CHALLENGES
  • Political Inaction
  • EconomicInterests
  • Technological Limitations.

SOLUTIONS
    Mitigation strategies: Develop and enforce policies to reduce carbon emissions.
    Climate education and awareness: Engage communities in climate action efforts.
