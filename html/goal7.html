GOAL 7

AFFORDABLE AND CLEAN ENERGY

AIM

This goal aims to ensure universal access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy sources. It promotes the use of renewable energy, energy efficiency, and infrastructure development. The goal highlights the importance of energy for economic development and aims to reduce reliance on fossil fuels.

       

TARGETS
  • 7.1 : Universal Access To Modern Energy
  • 7.2 : Increase Global Percentage Of Renewable Energy
  • 7.3 : Double The Improvement In Energy Efficiency
  • 7.4 : Promote Access To Research, Technology And Investments In Clean Energy
  • 7.5 : Expand And Upgrade Energy Services For Developing Countries

CHALLENGES
  • Poverty
  • Lack of Infrastructure
  • Dependence on Fossil Fuels.

SOLUTIONS
  • Renewable energy investment: Expand access to solar, wind, and other clean energy sources.
  • Energy efficiency programs: Encourage the adoption of energy-saving technologies.

Students as Changemakers: Ignite Your Passion to Fuel a Future of Affordable and Clean Energy Solutions
  • Raise awareness about renewable energy through school campaigns.
  • Promote energy conservation at school and home.
  • Join or start environmental clubs focused on clean energy initiatives.
  • Organize fundraising events to support clean energy projects.
  • Advocate for clean energy policies in your community.
