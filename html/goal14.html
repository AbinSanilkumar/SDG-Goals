GOAL 14

LIFE BELOW WATER

AIM

This goal focuses on conserving and sustainably using the oceans, seas, and marine resources. It aims to address issues like marine pollution, overfishing, and ocean acidification. The goal emphasizes the importance of protecting marine biodiversity and promoting sustainable fishing practices.

       

TARGETS
  • 14.1: Reduce Marine Pollution.
  • 14.2: Protect and Restore Ecosystems.
  • 14.3: Reduce Ocean Acidification.
  • 14.4: Sustainable Fishing.
  • 14.5: Conserve Coastal and Marine Areas.
  • 14.6: End Subsidies Contributing to Overfishing.
  • 14.7: Increase the Economic Benefits From Sustainable Use of Marine Resources.
  • 14.8: Increase Scientific Knowledge, Research and Technology for Ocean Health.

CHALLENGES
  • Overfishing, pollution, and climate change.

SOLUTIONS
    Marine conservation efforts: Establish protected marine areas and sustainable fishing practices.
    Reduce plastic pollution: Implement bans on single-use plastics and promote alternatives.

Students as Changemakers: Ride the Wave of Change for Life Below Water
  • Raise awareness about ocean conservation through campaigns and events.
  • Participate in beach cleanups to remove plastic and debris.
  • Promote sustainable practices and reduce plastic use.
  • Support marine conservation organizations through volunteering or fundraising.
  • Educate yourself and others about local marine life.
