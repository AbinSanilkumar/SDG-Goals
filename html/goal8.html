GOAL 8

DECENT WORK AND ECONOMIC GROWTH

AIM

Decent Work and Economic Growth seeks to promote inclusive economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all. It emphasizes job creation, equal pay, and labor rights. The goal also focuses on supporting small and medium-sized enterprises and fostering innovation and entrepreneurship.

TARGETS
  • 8.1: Sustainable Economic Growth.
  • 8.2: Diversify, Innovate and Upgrade for Economic Productivity.
  • 8.3: Promote Policies to Support Job Creation and Growing Enterprises.
  • 8.4: Improve Resource Efficiency in Consumption and Production.
  • 8.5: Full Employment and Decent Work With Equal Pay.
  • 8.6: Promote Youth Employment, Education and Training.
  • 8.7: End Modern Slavery, Trafficking and Child Labour.
  • 8.8: Protect Labour Rights and Promote Safe Working Environments.
  • 8.9: Promote Beneficial and Sustainable Tourism.
  • 8.10: Universal Access to Banking, Insurance and Financial Services.

CHALLENGES
  • Economic Inequality
  • Globalization
  • Automation.

SOLUTIONS
  • Promote fair labor practices: Enforce labor rights and provide job training programs.
  • Support innovation and entrepreneurship: Create incubators and funding for startups.
