GOAL 6

CLEAN WATER AND SANITATION

AIM

Clean Water and Sanitation ensures that everyone has access to safe drinking water and adequate sanitation. The goal emphasizes the sustainable management of water resources, improving water quality, and promoting hygiene practices. It addresses issues like water scarcity and the protection of freshwater ecosystems.

       

TARGETS
  • 6.1: Safe and Affordable Drinking Water.
  • 6.2: End Open Defecation and Provide Access to Sanitation and Hygiene.
  • 6.3: Improve Water Quality, Wastewater Treatment and Safe Reuse.
  • 6.4: Increase Water-Use Efficiency and Ensure Freshwater Supplies.
  • 6.5: Implement Integrated Water Resources Management.
  • 6.6: Protect and Restore Water-Related Ecosystems.
  • 6.7: Expand Water and Sanitation Support to Developing Countries.

CHALLENGES
  • Lack of Infrastructure
  • Pollution
  • Climate Change.

SOLUTIONS
  • Invest in water infrastructure: Build and maintain sustainable water supply systems.
  • Promote hygiene education: Implement community programs on sanitation and hygiene practices.
