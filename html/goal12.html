GOAL 12

RESPONSIBLE CONSUMPTION AND PRODUCTION

AIM

Responsible Consumption and Production encourages sustainable practices in consumption and production patterns. It aims to reduce waste, improve resource efficiency, and promote sustainable business practices. The goal emphasizes the importance of sustainable lifestyles and the need to reduce the ecological footprint.

TARGETS
  • 12.1 : Implement the 10-year sustainable consumption and production framework
  • 12.2 : Sustainable management and use of natural resources
  • 12.3 : Halve global per capita food waste
  • 12.4 : Responsible management of chemicals and waste
  • 12.5 : Substantially reduce waste generation
  • 12.6 : Encourage companies to adopt sustainable practices and sustainability reporting
  • 12.7 : Promote sustainable public procurement practices
  • 12.8 : Promote universal understanding of sustainable lifestyles
  • 12.9 : Support developing countriesâ€™ scientific and technological capacity for sustainable consumption and production

CHALLENGES
  • Consumerism
  • Lack of Awareness
  • Environmental Degradation

SOLUTIONS
  • Promote circular economy practices: Encourage recycling and waste reduction initiatives.
  • Sustainable supply chains: Work with businesses to implement ethical sourcing practices.

Students as Changemakers: Unleash Your Influence to Champion Responsible Consumption and Transform Production Practices

  • Encourage recycling and waste reduction in schools.
