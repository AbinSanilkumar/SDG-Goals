GOAL 15

LIFE ON LAND

AIM

Life on Land aims to protect, restore, and promote the sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems. It addresses deforestation, land degradation, and biodiversity loss. The goal emphasizes the importance of sustainable forest management, combating desertification, and conserving natural habitats.

       

TARGETS
  • 15.1: Conserve and Restore Terrestrial and Freshwater Ecosystems.
  • 15.2: End Deforestation and Restore Degraded Forests.
  • 15.3: End Desertification and Restore Degraded Land.
  • 15.4: Ensure Conservation of Mountain Ecosystems.
  • 15.5: Protect Biodiversity and Natural Habitats.
  • 15.6: Promote Access to Genetic Resources and Fair Sharing of the Benefits.
  • 15.7: Eliminate Poaching and Trafficking of Protected Species.
  • 15.8: Special and Differential Treatment for Developing Countries.

CHALLENGES
  • Deforestation
  • Land Degradation
  • Biodiversity Loss

SOLUTIONS
    Afforestation and reforestation: Implement programs to restore ecosystems and biodiversity.
    Sustainable land management: Promote practices that protect and sustainably manage land resources.

Students as Changemakers: Unleash Your Potential for a Greener Tomorrow
  • Raise awareness about biodiversity and terrestrial ecosystems through campaigns.
  • Participate in tree planting initiatives to combat deforestation.
  • Promote recycling and waste reduction in your community.
  • Support local conservation efforts by volunteering.
  • Engage in community cleanups in parks and neighborhoods.
  • Learn about and share knowledge of local flora and fauna.
