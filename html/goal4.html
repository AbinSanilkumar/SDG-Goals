GOAL 4

QUALITY EDUCATION

AIM

Quality Education seeks to provide inclusive and equitable education at all levels, from early childhood to higher education. It emphasizes lifelong learning opportunities, ensuring that all individuals acquire the necessary skills for employment and active citizenship. The goal addresses educational inequalities, focusing on marginalized groups, and promotes education for sustainable development.

       

TARGETS
  • 4.1 : Free Primary and Secondary Education.
  • 4.2 : Equal Access to Quality Pre-primary Education.
  • 4.3 : Equal Access to Affordable Technical, Vocational and Higher Education.
  • 4.4 : Increase the Number of People With Relevant Skills for Financial Success.
  • 4.5 : Eliminate All Discrimination in Education.
  • 4.6 : Universal Literacy and Numeracy.
  • 4.7 : Education for Sustainable Development and Global Citizenship.

CHALLENGES
  • Lack of access to education, especially for girls and in conflict areas.

SOLUTIONS
  • Inclusive education systems: Provide scholarships and resources for marginalized groups.
  • Teacher training and curriculum development: Focus on critical thinking and digital literacy.

Students as Changemakers: Ignite Your Power to Transform Education for All
  • Participate actively in class and school discussions.
  • Advocate for equal access to resources and opportunities.
  • Collaborate with teachers to enhance curriculum and teaching methods.
  • Organize peer tutoring and study groups.
  • Join or lead student councils to influence educational policies.
  • Promote inclusive learning by supporting diverse student needs.
