GOAL 5

GENDER EQUALITY

AIM

This goal aims to eliminate discrimination and violence against women and girls. It promotes equal opportunities in leadership and decision-making and seeks to ensure access to education, health care, and economic resources. It also emphasizes the importance of sexual and reproductive rights and aims to challenge harmful gender norms.

TARGETS
  • 5.1: End Discrimination Against Women and Girls.
  • 5.2: End All Violence Against and Exploitation of Women and Girls.
  • 5.3: Eliminate Forced Marriages and Genital Mutilation.
  • 5.4: Value Unpaid Care and Promote Shared Domestic Responsibilities.
  • 5.5: Ensure Full Participation in Leadership and Decision-Making.
  • 5.6: Mobilize Resources to Implement Policies to End Poverty. Universal Access to Reproductive Health and Rights.
  • 5.7: Equal Rights to Economic Resources, Property Ownership, and Financial Services.

CHALLENGES
  • Cultural and Societal Norms
  • Economic Inequality
  • Violence Against Women

SOLUTIONS
  • Empower women economically: Support entrepreneurship and access to capital.
  • Combat gender-based violence: Implement laws and awareness campaigns to protect women.

Students as Changemakers: Redefining Norms, Embracing Equality
  • Organize campaigns on gender issues.
  • Push for inclusive school policies.
  • Establish support groups for discussions.
  • Encourage gender-neutral terms.
  • Advocate for diverse participation in activities.
  • Partner with diverse groups on initiatives.
  • Highlight gender equality advocates online.
